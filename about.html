ABOUT US

The Celebration Tales

At The Celebration Tales , we believe every story deserves to be told beautifully. Our passion lies in capturing emotions, moments, and memories that live far beyond the celebrations themselves.

Founded by Sangmesh Mehtre and Datta Kale, we are more than just filmmakers – we are storytellers who weave together sights, sounds, and feelings into timeless films and photographs.

From birthdays to cultural festivals and personal milestones, we craft every project with artistry, heart, and dedication. What makes us unique is the way we immerse ourselves in your journey, ensuring that every detail, every laugh, and every tear is preserved for generations to come.

We don’t just document your day – we transform it into a cinematic masterpiece.

Datta Kale

"Every celebration is unique, and documenting real moments in a creative and artful way motivates us every day."

We are two cousins who grew up sharing not only family, but also the same dream — to tell stories that touch hearts. What started as a small idea of capturing special moments slowly turned into a passion that became our life’s work.

We believe that every event is more than just a gathering. It is a collection of emotions — the laughter of friends, the tears of loved ones, the nervous smiles, and the love that shines through it all. These are the moments that matter, and these are the moments we live to capture.

Our journey with The Celebration Tales is built on this simple truth — that love, joy, and togetherness deserve to be remembered in the most beautiful way. Every film and photograph we create is a piece of your story, preserved forever with honesty and emotion.
