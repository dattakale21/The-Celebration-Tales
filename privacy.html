PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: October 2, 2025

At The Celebration Tales , we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and event details.
  • Photos, videos, and other media captured during events.
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely through third-party providers).
  • Technical data like IP address, browser type, and website usage (via analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide photography, videography, and related services for your events.
  • To communicate with you regarding bookings, updates, or inquiries.
  • To deliver your films, photographs, and albums.
  • To improve our services and website experience.
  • For marketing purposes (with your consent).

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Only authorized team members have access to your data for service-related purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information:

  • With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment gateways, cloud storage).
  • With your consent, for publishing selected images/videos for promotional purposes.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or updates to your data.
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations).
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
